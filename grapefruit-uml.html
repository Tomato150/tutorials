Grapefruitjs UML Diagrams

High Level Class Diagram

These are the key classes in understanding grapefruitjs.

A Game has many GameStates but, a game can only be in one of those states, stored in the activeState. Interaction is evented through InputManager. The Camera provides visual effects and the viewport into the world. The AudioManager provides auditory effects. The PhysicsSystem mixes in physics traits into sprites. The world is a TiledMap comprised of one or more layers. A TiledObjectGroup layer reprepsents entities like players, creeps and neutrals. A TileLayer is the terrain made of orthogonal or isometric tiles.
